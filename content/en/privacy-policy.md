### Tracking Technologies & Cookies 🍪

We roll with Cookies and similar tracking technologies to keep an eye on activity on our Service and stash away certain info. These tracking technologies include beacons, tags, and scripts, all used to collect and track information, plus jazz up and analyze our Service. The tech we use might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a tiny file dropped onto your device. You can tell your browser to give all Cookies the boot or to alert you when a Cookie is being sent. But, if you don't play ball with Cookies, you might not be able to use some parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to reject Cookies, our Service might just use them anyway.

- **Web Beacons**: Web Beacons are tiny electronic files (also known as clear GIFs, pixel tags, and single-pixel GIFs) that certain parts of our Service and our emails might contain. These little guys let the Company (for instance) count users who've visited those pages or opened an email, and they're used for other cool website stats (like noting a section's popularity and checking the system and server's integrity).

Cookies can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. When you go offline, Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device, while Session Cookies get wiped out as soon as you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the following reasons:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super crucial for dishing out the services available through the Website and letting you use some of its awesome features. They help verify user identity and put the brakes on fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you're asking for just wouldn't be possible, and we only use them to provide those services to you.

- **Cookies Policy / Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are all about remembering if users have given a thumbs-up to the use of Cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you're cruising the Website, like recalling your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to serve up a more personalized experience, saving you the hassle of re-entering your preferences every single time you hit up the Website.

For more info about the Cookies we use and your options regarding them, go check out our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
