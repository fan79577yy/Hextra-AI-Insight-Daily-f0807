### Tracking Technologies and Cookies

Cookies and similar tracking technologies are what we use to keep tabs on activity on our service and stash some info. The tracking tech we roll with includes beacons, tags, and scripts – all for collecting and tracking info, and totally to amp up and analyze our service. The tech we might use includes:

- **Cookies (or Browser Cookies):** A Cookie is a small file chilling on your device. You can tell your browser to totally reject all Cookies or give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But hey, if you don't accept Cookies, you might not be able to use some parts of our service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to reject Cookies, our service will likely use them.
- **Web Beacons:** Some parts of our service and our emails might have tiny electronic files called Web Beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These awesome little files let the Company (for example) count users who have bopped to those pages or opened an email, and they're used for other cool website stats too (like recording a section's popularity and verifying system and server integrity).

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device even when you're offline, while Session Cookies totally vanish the second you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for these purposes:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type:** Session Cookies
  - **Administered by:** Us
  - **Purpose:** These Cookies are super crucial to give you the services available through the Website and to let you use some of its features. They help authenticate users and prevent sketchy use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for can't be provided, and we only use these Cookies to hook you up with those services.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type:** Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by:** Us
  - **Purpose:** These Cookies are all about figuring out if users have accepted the use of cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type:** Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by:** Us
  - **Purpose:** These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you're using the Website, like recalling your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you from having to re-enter your preferences every single time you hit up the Website.

For more info on the Cookies we use and your options regarding them, zoom on over to our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
